Brand Philosophy

At JoiMirae, we believe that true learning begins with curiosity and flourishes through play. Playful learning is designed around each child, creating experiences that are engaging, challenging, and joyful, helping children of all ages develop grit, resilience, and creativity.

Through immersive experiences, play-based exploration, and personalised learning insights, we help children build confidence, curiosity, and courage, nurturing both heart and mind.

品牌理念

在卓敏源，我们相信真正的学习始于好奇，并在探索与游戏中茁壮成长。寓教于乐因每个孩子而异，通过趣味与挑战相结合的体验，帮助孩子在各个成长阶段培养坚毅、韧性与创造力。
