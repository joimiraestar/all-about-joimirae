How We Use Your Data

  • Process and fulfill your orders

We send updates about new products, special offers, and Joimirae news - but only if you choose to. Here's how:

  • We will never sell or share your email/phone with anyone else

  • You can request to see what data we have about you

  • You can opt out of marketing emails/SMS at any time
  • You choose what information to share - nothing is mandatory except what's needed to process your order
