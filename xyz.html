How It Works

Three Steps to Chinese Fun

No setup fuss. No boring drills. Just race, match, and win.

🃏

Step 1

Shuffle & Deal

Lay out the cards face up. Every player scans the pile — the hunt is on.

⚡

Step 2

Race to Match

Spot the character before your opponent. Fastest brain takes the card.

✍️

Step 3

Write, Say, Own It

After the race, write the word, say it aloud, use it in a sentence. It sticks!

Brand Philosophy

At JoiMirae, we believe that true learning begins with curiosity and flourishes through play. Playful learning is designed around each child, creating experiences that are engaging, challenging, and joyful, helping children of all ages develop grit, resilience, and creativity.

Through immersive experiences, play-based exploration, and personalised learning insights, we help children build confidence, curiosity, and courage, nurturing both heart and mind.

Our Offerings

  • 🏕️Holiday Camps: experiential learning through play and teamwork
  • 🧭Consultancy: strength-based learning habit insights for parents and children, designed for ages 11 to 15
  • 🎮Games and Learning Tools: playful pathways to curiosity and growth

At JoiMirae, learning shines from within. Joyful, purposeful, and courageous.

品牌理念

在卓敏源，我们相信真正的学习始于好奇，并在探索与游戏中茁壮成长。寓教于乐因每个孩子而异，通过趣味与挑战相结合的体验，帮助孩子在各个成长阶段培养坚毅、韧性与创造力。

通过实践营沉浸式体验、寓教于乐的探索方式以及个性化的学习洞察，我们帮助孩子建立自信、好奇心与勇气，同时兼顾心智与品格的发展。

我们的主要项目包括：

  • 🏕️假期营：通过游戏和团队合作实现体验式学习
  • 🧭学习咨询：为家长和孩子提供优势导向的学习习惯指导，适合 11 至 15 岁的孩子
  • 🎮学习游戏与工具：通过趣味方式激发孩子的好奇心与成长潜能

在卓敏源，学习是一种自然的喜悦，是探索目标、好奇与勇气的旅程。
