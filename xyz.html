IB vs IP Programme Consulting

Confused between International Baccalaureate (IB) and Integrated Programme (IP)? You're not alone. This is one of the most important educational decisions you'll make, and we're here to help you choose the right path.

Understanding Your Options

Aspect IB Programme IP Programme
Structure International curriculum with emphasis on critical thinking Local curriculum with accelerated pace
Flexibility More subject choices, interdisciplinary approach More focused curriculum, streamlined path
Workload High, with emphasis on extended essays and projects Very high, fast-paced with frequent assessments
Best For Students who like diverse subjects and conceptual learning Students who thrive with structure and local focus
