Data Protection & Privacy

Your data, your choice. We only collect information you choose to share with us.

What We Collect

When you contact us or fill out our forms, you decide what information to provide:

  • Email: For order updates and shipping notifications
  • Phone: Optional - only if you want SMS updates
  • Country: To process your order (auto-detected, you can change it)
  • Level preferences: To know which Wordmatchic Cards interest you

How We Use Your Data

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send shipping and order updates
  • Contact you about your inquiry (email or phone, your choice)
  • Improve our products based on feedback
  • Marketing & Newsletter: Only if you opt-in via our confirmation email or directly ask us

Marketing & Newsletter Opt-In

We send updates about new products, special offers, and Joimirae news - but only if you choose to. Here's how:

  • When you submit an order form, we'll ask if you want SMS updates in our confirmation email
  • We'll also offer to add you to our newsletter for product announcements
  • You can unsubscribe anytime by replying "STOP" to SMS or clicking unsubscribe in any email
  • We will never sell or share your email/phone with anyone else

Your Rights

  • You can request to see what data we have about you
  • You can ask us to delete your information anytime
  • You can opt out of marketing emails/SMS at any time
  • You choose what information to share - nothing is mandatory except what's needed to process your order

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Forms and Google Sheets. We don't share your information with third parties. We comply with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012.
